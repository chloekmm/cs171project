Grey Dot Indicators

Visual feedback system for clickable desk artifacts

Dot States

1. Default (Pulsing)

Unclicked artifacts
The dot pulses gently to draw attention to unexplored items

2. Hover

On mouse hover
Dot scales up and brightens to indicate interactivity

3. Clicked (Semi-transparent)

After clicking
Dot becomes semi-transparent to show it's been explored

Dot Positions on Artifacts

Each dot is positioned in the top-right corner of its respective artifact:

  • US Map: Top 10%, Right 10%
  • Laptop: Top 8%, Right 12%
  • Books: Top 8%, Right 15%
  • Paper: Top 10%, Right 10%
  • Graduation Cap: Top 15%, Right 20%
